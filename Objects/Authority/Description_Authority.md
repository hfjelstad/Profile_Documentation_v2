# Authority

**Description:**
The **Authority** element in NeTEx represents a public transport authority responsible for planning, organizing, and managing public transport services within a specific area. It typically oversees Operators and ensures compliance with regulations and service standards.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Defines service requirements and policies.
- Contracts Operators to deliver services.
- Manages fare structures and ticketing systems.

**Relations to Other Elements:**
- **Operator:** Authorities contract Operators to provide services.
- **ResponsibilitySet:** Defines roles and responsibilities for Authorities and Operators.
